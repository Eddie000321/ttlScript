⚠ Password Recovery / Factory Reset — Password unknown / Locked out
Router Password Recovery ISR / ASR
  • Turn OFF the router.
  • Turn it ON.
  • Immediately press Ctrl + Break (or use Break button if available) repeatedly.
  • Wait until you see rommon 1 >.
  • Then click Start Recovery below.
Switch Factory Reset 2960 / 3560 / 3750
  • Unplug the power cable.
  • Press & Hold the MODE button (Light Gray Button).
  • Plug power back in (keep holding MODE).
  • Wait ~15s until switch: appears.
9200 Series Recovery Catalyst 9200/9300
  • Unplug power. Hold MODE button (Light Gray Button).
  • Reconnect power. Hold MODE until switch: prompt.
  • This process takes about 10 minutes (full boot).
